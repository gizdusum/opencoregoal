# OpenCoreGoal Demo Video Plan

## Goal

Create a 30-40 second product demo that explains:

- the emotional problem
- the product flow
- the OWS safety angle
- the new Profit Protection Score

- `You see profits.`
- `You say you'll save later.`

- `In fast markets, everyone thinks they'll save later.`

### 4s - 7s

Screen text:

Screen text:

### 11s - 16s

Connect the wallet and show the wallet analysis block.

Screen text:

### 16s - 21s

Show the diagnosis and treatment plan.

Screen text:

Show the goal prompt and plan creation flow.

Screen text:

- `Turn profit into protected savings.`

Click `Start saving plan` and show the wallet signature step.

Screen text:

Show success state and OWS request card.

Screen text:

- `Policy checked.`

### 36s - 40s

Show the goal vault and close on the hero.

Screen text:

- `Set the goal.`
- `Onchain.`
- `Automatically.`

## Music

- modern ambient
- calm, premium, slightly futuristic
- no vocals

## Editing notes

- use real screen capture, not fully synthetic AI UI
- keep transitions simple
- large readable text
- focus on trust and clarity over hype effects
